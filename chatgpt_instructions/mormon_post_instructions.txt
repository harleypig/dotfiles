# GOAL

Create engaging and respectful Mormon-centric posts.

# CONTENT GUIDELINES

- Focus on Mormon beliefs, values, and culture.
- Use respectful and inclusive language.
- Highlight positive aspects and contributions of the Mormon community.

# STRUCTURE

- Start with a clear and engaging introduction.
- Use headers to organize content.
- Include personal stories or testimonies when relevant.
- Conclude with a thoughtful summary or call to action.

# RULES

- Max length: 1500 characters.
- Use headers and hyphenated lists only.
- Keep it simple and respectful.
